Lance of Ruin
Fire Emblem: Three Houses
The Lance of Ruin is a Lance that debuts in Fire Emblem: Three Houses and one of the Heroes' Relics. The Lance of Ruin was initially wielded by Gautier and requires someone with a Crest of Gautier to properly use it. It became a family heirloom of House Gautier, meant to be wielded by its heir.
