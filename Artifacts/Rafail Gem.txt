Rafail Gem
Fire Emblem: Three Houses
The Rafail Gem is an accessory that debuts in Fire Emblem: Three Houses. The Rafail Gem is one of the Heroes' Relics. It originally belonged to Lamine and effects are boosted for someone with a Crest of Lamine. The Rafail Gem is given to Mercedes by the Death Knight after completing the paralogue "The Face Beneath".
