The Surprisingly Effective Dagger
Unbroken
The Surprisingly Effective Dagger, also referred to as the Surprisingly Ineffective Dagger, is an extremely useless sentient magical dagger the party found that trades half of a creature's max hitpoints each round for extra damage.
