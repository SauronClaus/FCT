Piko Piko Hammer
Sonic the Hedgehog
The Piko Piko Hammer (ピコピコハンマー PikoPiko Hanmā?), also called the Magical Hammer and Love Hammer, is an object that appears in the Sonic the Hedgehog series. It is a large yellow and red hammer and Amy Rose's signature weapon, which she uses as a melee weapon and to perform special techniques.
