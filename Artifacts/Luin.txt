Lúin
Fire Emblem: Three Houses
Lúin is a Lance that debuts in Fire Emblem: Three Houses. It is one of the Heroes' Relics that has been passed down through the Daphnel bloodline. When used by someone who bears the Crest of Daphnel, the Combat Art Burning Quake may be used.
