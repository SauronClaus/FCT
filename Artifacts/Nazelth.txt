Nazelth the Ever Hungry
Unbroken
Nazelth the Ever Hungry was a magical weapon that hid inside the black ley line of Aspen. Once a powerful wizard that experimented with absorbing power from magical weapons, a wild magic surge occured one day during his experiments and he was changed into a magical weapon with only his thirst for more power remaining.
