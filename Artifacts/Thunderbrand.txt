Thunderbrand
Fire Emblem: Three Houses
Thunderbrand is a sword that first appears in Fire Emblem: Three Houses and is one of the Heroes' Relics. A relic of House Charon, its current wielder is Catherine who used it as far back as her time as Thunderstrike Cassandra.
