Aymr
Fire Emblem: Three Houses
Aymr is an Axe that debuts in Fire Emblem: Three Houses. Despite its appearance, it is not considered part of the Heroes' Relics but instead a Crest Stone weapon that was designed specifically for Edelgard's use.
