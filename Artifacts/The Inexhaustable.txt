The Inexhaustable
Fire Emblem: Three Houses
The Inexhaustible is a bow that debuts in Fire Emblem: Three Houses. It is a Sacred Weapon associated with Indech. The wielder can attack twice and it restores HP every turn, with someone bearing a Crest of Indech recovering more HP.
