The Crown of Horns
Bone
Crown of Horns is a sacred object hidden in Tanen Gard and the dragons most guarded secret. It destroyed the Lord of the Locust when Thorn and Fone Bone used the parts of his body that they had to tip the Waking World and the Dreaming back into balance.
