Magneto's Helmet
Marvel Comics
Magneto's Helmet is the helmet made by Magneto. An iconic helmet worn by him, the helmet protects its wearer from all but the strongest or most unexpected of telepathic attacks. This is achieved due to technology wired into the helmet itself.
