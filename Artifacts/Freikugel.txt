Freikugel
Fire Emblem: Three Houses
Freikugel is an Axe that debuts in Fire Emblem: Three Houses. It is one of the Heroes' Relics, passed down through the bloodline of Goneril, one of the Ten Elites. When used by someone who bears the Crest of Goneril, the Combat Art Apocalyptic Flame may be used.
