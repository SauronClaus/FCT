Crusher (Fire Emblem)
Fire Emblem: Three Houses
Crusher is an Axe that debuts in Fire Emblem: Three Houses. It is one of the Heroes' Relics, passed down through the bloodline of Dominic, one of the Ten Elites. When used by someone who bears the Crest of Dominic, the Combat Art Dust may be used.
