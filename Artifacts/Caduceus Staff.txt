Caduceus Staff
Fire Emblem: Three Houses
The Caduceus Staff is a staff in Fire Emblem: Three Houses. It is a Sacred Weapon associated with the Crest of Cethleann. It can be equipped to increase Magic attacks range by 1. It also restores HP every turn. The effect is increased for someone who bears the Crest of Cethleann.
