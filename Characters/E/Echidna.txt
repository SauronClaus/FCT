Echidna
Worm
Echidna (pronounced “eh-kid-nah”), formerly known as Noelle Meinhardt, is a powerful being described as a potential Endbringer. She is amazingly destructive, powerful, and hard to stop. With her ability to create clones and extraordinary durability, she qualifies as a Class-S threat, a category that includes the likes of the Endbringers.
