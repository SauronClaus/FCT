Eda Clawthorne
The Owl House
Edalyn "Eda" Clawthorne is the deuteragonist of The Owl House. She is a rebellious witch who was cursed as a teenager by her older sister, Lilith, before running away from home and becoming the most wanted and powerful wild witch in the Boiling Isles.
