Nico di Angelo
Percy Jackson
Nico di Angelo is a 15-year-old Italian-born Greek demigod, the son of Hades and Maria di Angelo. He's also the younger brother of the late Bianca di Angelo and the paternal half-brother of Hazel Levesque. He's known in the Underworld as the "Ghost King" after taking the title from Minos in The Battle of the Labyrinth. He is currently in a relationship with Will Solace.
