Noelle Holiday
Deltarune
Noelle Holiday is a main character in Deltarune. She has a minor role in Chapter 1 and is a temporary ally in Chapter 2. Noelle is one of Kris's classmates, as well as their neighbor and childhood friend. She lives on the northwest side of Hometown with her mother, the Mayor, and her father, Rudy, who is currently in the hospital with an unnamed illness.
