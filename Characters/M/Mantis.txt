Mantis
Marvel Comics
Mantis is a fictional character primarily appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The character has been depicted as a member of the Avengers and the Guardians of the Galaxy, as the bride of Kang the Conqueror, and as the mother of Sequoia.
