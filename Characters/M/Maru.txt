Maru
Stardew Valley
Maru is a villager who lives in The Mountains north of Pelican Town. She's one of the twelve characters available to marry. She lives north of town with her family in a house attached to Robin's carpenter shop. In the Social Status menu, Maru's outfit will change to a nursing uniform when she is at her job at the clinic.
