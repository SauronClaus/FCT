Irey West
DC Comics
Iris "Irey" West is a fictional superheroine known as Impulse and Kid Flash in the DC Comics universe. She is the daughter of Flash (Wally West) and Linda Park-West. She first appeared in Kingdom Come #3 (July 1996).
