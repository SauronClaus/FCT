Walt Stone
Kane Chronicles
Walt Stone was originally one of the new trainees that Sadie Kane and Carter Kane were training along with Jaz and originally came from Seattle, Washington. He is known as a sau; a charm maker. He is one of Sadie's two love interests and as of The Serpent's Shadow, Anubis' first human host. Walt/Anubis is currently in a relationship with Sadie Kane.
