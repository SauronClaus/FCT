Dabi
My Hero Academia
Dabi (荼毘 Dabi?), real name Toya Todoroki (轟燈矢 Todoroki Tōya?), is a major antagonist in the My Hero Academia manga and anime series. He is a Villain affiliated with the League of Villains, and a former member of the organization's since disbanded Vanguard Action Squad. He and the rest of his team serve as the main antagonists of the Forest Training Camp Arc. He also serves as an antagonist in the Pro Hero Arc.
