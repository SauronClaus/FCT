Dozer
The Matrix
Dozer was a crew member of the Nebuchadnezzar who appeared to do double duty as its pilot and in a health and wellness capacity. He was born naturally in Zion, brother of Tank and Zee, and died aboard the ship. He was survived by his widow, Cas, and their two children. He was played by Anthony Ray Parker.
