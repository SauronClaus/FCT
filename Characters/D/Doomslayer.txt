Doom Slayer
Doom
The Doom Slayer is the protagonist and playable character of Doom, Doom II: Hell on Earth, Final Doom, Doom 64, Doom (2016) and Doom Eternal. He has also guest starred in the Quake games such as Quake Champions and Quake III Arena.
