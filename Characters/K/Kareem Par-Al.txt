Kareem Par-Al
Band of Fools Original
Kareem Par-Al was a half-elf Bard (College of Lore) associated with the original Band of Fools. They found her in the Redcloak's Dungeon as a prisoner, whereupon after freeing her she joined the party as a full-fledged member, through the beginnings of the war against the Old Gods.
