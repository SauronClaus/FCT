Kalathos Sunstrider
Band of Fools Original
Kalathos Sunstrider was an elven evocation wizard (focused in pyromancy) that joined the Band of Fools as a member of the Original Three, before also joining the reboot. He originally joined the campaign as a member of the Original Three hired by Gundren Rockseeker to drive a wagon of supplies to Phandelver, before joining the group as they attempted to stop the Old Gods. In the second campaign, he was hired by Aelar Galanodel and Shabroth Nexoc to investigate the disappearance of their comrades before becoming embroiled in the events surrounding the death of Ao, the All-God.
