Karun Patel
MCU
Karun Patel (Hindi: करुण पटेल) is the personal valet and assistant of the Bollywood filmmaker and Eternal, Kingo, and also served as the cameraman for Kingo's personal vlogs. In 2023, Karun accompied Kingo as the Eternals reunited to stop the Emergence, bringing along several cameras to document the experience. Following the betrayal of the Eternal, Ikaris, Karun returned to India with Kingo.
