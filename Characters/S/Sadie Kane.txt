Sadie Kane
Kane Chronicles
Sadie Kane is the youngest child of Julius and Ruby Kane and the sister of Carter Kane. She is one of the most powerful descendants of the ancient Egyptian pharaohs to be born in centuries. She becomes one of the strongest magicians in the world along with her brother Carter and her uncle Amos. Sadie is currently in a relationship with Walt Stone and Anubis.
