Sisu
Raya and the Last Dragon
Sisu is the deuteragonist of Disney's 2021 animated feature film, Raya and the Last Dragon. For the majority of the film, she is the last of her kind. Sisu is a self-deprecating yet comedic water dragon, called upon by Raya to save humanity from a dark force known as the Druun.
