Sersi
MCU
Sersi is the Prime Eternal, and the current leader of Earth's Eternals. Created in the World Forge by Arishem the Judge, she was sent to Earth to protect humanity from the Deviants. While on her mission, she grew a close relationship with Ikaris and eventually married him. By 2023, about a century after Ikaris left her, she became a history professor at the Natural History Museum, and fell in love with Dane Whitman.
