Shuichi Iguchi (伊口秀一 Iguchi Shūichi?), also known as Spinner (スピナー Supinā?)[1] is a villain affiliated with the League of Villains and was part of the Vanguard Action Squad.[2] He and the rest of his team serve as the main antagonists of the Forest Training Camp Arc.
