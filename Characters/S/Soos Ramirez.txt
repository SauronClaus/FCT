Soos Ramírez
Gravity Falls
Jesus "Soos" Alzamirano Ramírez (born July 13, 1990) is an employee and current owner of the Mystery Shack. He was born in Gravity Falls, Oregon. He is an awkward young man who works at the Mystery Shack. The tritagonist of Gravity Falls, Dipper and Mabel Pines sometimes include Soos in their adventures. He is the Question Mark on The Zodiac.
