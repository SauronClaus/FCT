Shoto Todoroki
My Hero Academia
Shoto Todoroki (轟焦凍 Todoroki Shōto?), also known as Shoto (ショート Shōto?), is the tritagonist of the My Hero Academia manga and anime series. He is a student in Class 1-A at U.A. High School, where he got in through official recommendations and is training to become a Pro Hero. He is the youngest son of Endeavor, the No. 1 (formerly No. 2) Hero.
