Samirah al-Abbas
Magnus Chase
Samirah "Sam" al-Abbas is Magnus Chase's Valkyrie and the Muslim demigod daughter of Loki. She became a Valkyrie, a chooser of the slain, at fourteen when saving her school from an attacking frost giant. Since then she has faced aviaries with her friends and has won with their help. She is currently engaged to Amir Fadlan.
