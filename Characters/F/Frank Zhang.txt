Frank Zhang
Percy Jackson
Frank Zhang (also known as Fai Zhang by his Grandmother) is a Roman demigod, the son of Mars and Emily Zhang, as well as a legacy of Poseidon and Chloris. Frank is one of the seven demigods mentioned in the Prophecy of Seven and is the current male Praetor of the Twelfth Legion Fulminata. He is currently in a relationship with Praetor Hazel Levesque.
