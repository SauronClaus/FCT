Alyx Vance
Half-Life
Alyx Vance is the deuteragonist of Half-Life 2 and its Episodes and the titular protagonist of Half-Life: Alyx. A woman in her mid-twenties, she is a prominent figure in the Resistance campaign against the rule of the Combine.
