Anna Croft
Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint
Anna Croft is an American prophet who leads the organization Zarathustra. She was the only person with the attribute of 'prophet' in Ways of Survival and became Yoo Joonghyuk's primary rival in the second half of the novel.
