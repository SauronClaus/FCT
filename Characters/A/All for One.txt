All For One (オール・フォー・ワン Ōru Fō Wan?), surname Shigaraki (死柄木 Shigaraki?), is the overarching antagonist of the My Hero Academia manga and anime series. He is the leader and benefactor of the League of Villains, temporarily handing over the reins to Tomura Shigaraki prior to becoming imprisoned before taking them again during his second rise to power.
