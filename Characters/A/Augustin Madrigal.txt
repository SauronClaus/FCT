Agustín Madrigal
Encanto
Agustín Madrigal is a supporting character in Disney's 2021 animated feature film, Encanto. He is the bumbling, but well-meaning husband of Julieta Madrigal. Having married into the family, Agustín has no magical gift of his own. Despite this, his clumsy nature allows his wife not only to practice her gift, but to get a taste tester for her food.
