Akkar
Band of Fools (Original)
Akkar is a blind Wood-Elf Ranger, of the Hunter subclass, that joined the adventuring band during their travels after their battle at the Redcloak's hideout. Due to a wild surge, Akkar had a permanent Spider Climb cast upon him.
