Thorn Harvestar
Bone
Thorn Harvestar is the deuteragonist of the Bone series by Jeff Smith and a minor character in Quest for the Spark. Fone Bone developed a crush on her from the moment they met and the two became close friends. She lived with Gran'ma Ben at her farm for twelve years after leaving Atheia and returns to her birthplace in Treasure Hunters. In Eyes of the Storm, she is revealed to be the heir to the Atheian throne, and she takes the throne in the final volume of the series.
