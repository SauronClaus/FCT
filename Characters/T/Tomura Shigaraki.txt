Tomura Shigaraki
My Hero Academia
Tomura Shigaraki (死柄木弔 Shigaraki Tomura?), real name Tenko Shimura (志村転弧 Shimura Tenko?), is the main antagonist of the My Hero Academia manga and anime series. He is the leader of the League of Villains after his master, All For One's, defeat and arrest. He made his first major appearance as one of the main antagonists of the U.S.J. Arc. Like the rest of the League, his goal is to kill All Might, the "Symbol of Peace," thus destroying the society he represents and to create a new one with a new form of justice in his own image.
