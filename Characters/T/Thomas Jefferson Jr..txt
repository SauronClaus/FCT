Thomas Jefferson Jr.
Magnus Chase
Thomas Jefferson Jr. is a Norse demigod son of Tyr and a woman who escaped slavery. A veteran of the American Civil War, Jefferson became one of Odin's einherjar. He was born in Massachusetts, and was killed during the assault on Fort Wagner, South Carolina. After dying in battle he became an Einherjar.
