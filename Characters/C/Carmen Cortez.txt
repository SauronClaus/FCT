Carmen Cortez
Spy Kids
Carmen Elizabeth Juanita Echo Sky Brava Cortez is one of the main protagonists of the Spy Kids series. She is an agent of the OSS's Spy Kids Division, the daughter of Gregorio and Ingrid Cortez, and the older sister of Juni Cortez.
