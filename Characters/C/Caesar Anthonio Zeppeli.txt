Caesar Anthonio Zeppeli
JoJo's Bizarre Adventure: Battle Tendency
Caesar Anthonio Zeppeli (シーザー・アントニオ・ツェペリ Shīzā Аntonio Tseperi) is the deuteragonist featured in Battle Tendency. Caesar is an Italian Ripple user and disciple of Lisa Lisa. He is the grаndson of Will Anthonio Zeppeli and battles the Pillar Men alongside his allies and teachers to honor his heritage as a Zeppeli.
