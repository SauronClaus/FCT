Camilo Madrigal
Encanto
Camilo Madrigal is a supporting character in Disney's 2021 animated feature film Encanto. The middle child of Pepa and Félix Madrigal and proclaimed “theatre kid” of the family, Camilo was gifted with the ability to shape-shift.
