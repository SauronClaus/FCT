Zia Rashid
Kane Chronicles
Zia Rashid is a female Egyptian magician and a powerful scribe of the House of Life. She was the favored one of Ra and hosted Nephthys when Julius Kane freed the five children of Nut. She is the girlfriend of Carter Kane.
