Ralsei
Deltarune
Ralsei is a secondary protagonist of Deltarune and, presumably, the "Prince from the Dark" of the Heroes of Light, as foretold in the Prophecy. Having weaker attack and defense, and with the ability to cast healing spells, Ralsei acts as the party's healer.
