Himiko Toga
My Hero Academia
Himiko Toga (渡我被身子 Toga Himiko?) is a major antagonist of the My Hero Academia manga and anime series. She was a member of the League of Villains, affiliated with the Vanguard Action Squad. Himiko served as one of the main antagonists of the Forest Training Camp Arc. She is also featured as an antagonist during the Provisional Hero License Exam Arc and the Shie Hassaikai Arc.
