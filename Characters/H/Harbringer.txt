Harbringer
DC Comics
The Harbinger (Lyla Michaels) is a DC Comics superheroine created in the early 1980s. A version of Lyla Michaels appeared in The CW television series Arrow played by Audrey Marie Anderson as a recurring character. In the series, she is the director of A.R.G.U.S. and the wife of John Diggle. Michaels was also a recurring character on The Flash television series. Anderson's character temporarily became a more faithful version of the Harbinger for the Arrowverse crossover "Crisis on Infinite Earths".
