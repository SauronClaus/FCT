Bruce Ng
The Martian
Bruce Ng is an Asian-American man who is the Director of JPL (Jet Propulsion Laboratory) in California. He was the leader of the team that made the Iris I and the Iris II resupply probes as well as being present during the 'Project Elrond' meeting.
