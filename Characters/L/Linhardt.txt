Linhardt
Fire Emblem: Three Houses
Linhardt is a playable character in Fire Emblem: Three Houses. He is a student at the Officers Academy who is from the Adrestian Empire, and a member of the Black Eagles. He possesses a minor Crest of Saint Cethleann. He is 16 years old at the start of the game.
