Garrik Doriana was a male human warlock that grew up in the 12065 post-apocalyptic wasteland. He joined the party as a replacement for Lorana Ritten, who was killed by explosives while destroying a porcelain factory. Garrik Doriana also works as a minor government official within the Crater.
