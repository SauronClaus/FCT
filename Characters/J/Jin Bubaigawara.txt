Jin Bubaigawara (分倍河原仁 Bubaigawara Jin?), also known as Twice (トゥワイス Tuwaisu?), was a major antagonist of the My Hero Academia manga and anime series. He was a villain affiliated with the League of Villains and was part of its Vanguard Action Squad. He and the rest of his team served as the main antagonists of the Forest Training Camp Arc.
