Ochaco Uraraka
My Hero Academia
Ochaco Uraraka (麗日お茶子 Uraraka Ochako?), also known as Uravity (ウラビティ Urabiti?), is a student in Class 1-A at U.A. High School, training to become a Pro Hero. She is one of the main protagonists of My Hero Academia.
