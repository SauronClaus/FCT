Young Cyclops
Marvel Comics
Young Cyclops was a younger teenage version of Cyclops that got brought into the persent by Beast in the hopes of reminding Cyclops of why Xavier created the X-Men and prevent a mutant genocide. The original teenaged X-Men then decided they wanted to stay and become the All New X-Men.
