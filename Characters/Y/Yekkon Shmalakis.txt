Yekkon Shmalakis
Unbroken
Yekkon Shmalakis was a male wood elf warlock and one of four scarred that arrived in Unbroken in 12034. He eventually was merged with his sword, Osiris, in 12065 after a massive explosion. The resulting Yekkosiris was then wielded by Arrolys Larskano.
