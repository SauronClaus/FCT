Band of Fools (original)
The Band of Fools (original) campaign was the first Band of Fools campaign, running from circa 2018-2020. The campaign featured a multitude of adventurers as they embarked on a campaign to first recover the dragon amulets before being embroiled in a multiversal war after the old gods returned, forever shifting the balance of the multiverse.
