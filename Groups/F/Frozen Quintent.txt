The Frozen Quintet is the main characters from the Disney Film Frozen. Frozen is a 2013 American 3D computer-animated adventure musical fantasy comedy-drama film, created and produced at Walt Disney Animation Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures. Inspired by the Hans Christian Andersen fairy tale, The Snow Queen, it is the 53rd animated feature in the Disney Animated Canon.
