High Five (Ready Player One)
Ready Player One
The High Five comprises the first five individual gunters to obtain the Copper Key and clear the First Gate, along with finding the Jade and Crystal Key to open the Second and Third Gate. Unaffiliated with any Gunter Clan, they only banded together for their safety from IOI's malicious intents.
