Best Friends Squad
She-Ra and the Princesses of Power
The Best Friends Squad (also known as Best Friend Quad) is a group that portrays the friendship between Adora, Glimmer, Bow and eventually Catra. It is the core group of the series, which the plot revolves around. They are the central protagonists. After Horde Prime was defeated, they decide to go on a "Road Trip" to return magic to the universe.
