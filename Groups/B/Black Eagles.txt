Black Eagles
Fire Emblem: Three Houses
The Black Eagles (アドラークラッセ Adorā Kurasse lit. Adler Class) are one of the three student houses at the Garreg Mach Monastery Officers Academy in Fire Emblem: Three Houses. At Rhea's invitation, Byleth must choose between the Black Eagles and the other two houses, the Blue Lions and the Golden Deer, to personally teach.
