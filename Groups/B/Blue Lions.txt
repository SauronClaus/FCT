Blue Lions
Fire Emblem: Three Houses
The Blue Lions (ルーヴェンクラッセ Rūven Kurasse lit. Löwen Class) are one of the three student houses at the Garreg Mach Monastery Officers Academy in Fire Emblem: Three Houses. At Rhea's invitation, Byleth must choose between the Blue Lions and the other two houses, the Black Eagles and the Golden Deer, to personally teach.
