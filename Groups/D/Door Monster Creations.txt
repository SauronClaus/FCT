Door Monster
Door Monster Entertainment is an american entertainment Youtube channel located mainly in Austin, Texas. The channel is lead by Kyle Sullivan and mostly features comedy sketches, though it has also created a mini-series called The Guards Themselves. It usually releases sketches weekly (every Monday). The channel also features a comment-answering section every Friday.
